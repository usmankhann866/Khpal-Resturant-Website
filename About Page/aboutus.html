About Us

Our History

Started in 2021, Khpal Food combines flavors & inspiration from the Far East & the West to create what we think is the best! Home to the original (recipe), we specialize in pizzas, as well as handmade appetizers, sandwiches, salads, and gluten friendly, vegetarian, & vegan selections. Feel free to indulge in a tiki drink or craft beer with our fantastic unique cuisine. Cheers!
The Khpal Food, a successful chain started by our CEO, Usman Khan, that featured for the first time the world's best Dampukh.

Corporate Leadership

Usman Khan Chief Executive Officer

Our CEO, Usman Khan, credits his hardworking, who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of Pakistan with the intention of giving their children the best future. My mother's whipping up the tastiest dishes with whatever is available inexpensively at the supermarket, was his first inspiration to create theDampukh became well known. He brings his zeal for dasi food.

Nadir Shah Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Taneem Ullah Jan, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in farming and produce. His experiences growing up on a farm in the Australian outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that runs, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Hassan Babar Chief Taste Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, Nadir Shah, our CTO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

Taneem Ullah Jan Executive Chief

Award winning three-star Michelin chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the culinary world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering Indo-Italian fusion experiences. He says, Put together the cuisines from the two craziest cultures, and you get a winning hit!
